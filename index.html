About Me




Hi! I'm a passionate final-year B.Tech Computer Science student from Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education, originally from Khammam, Telangana. I come from a humble background and have always been driven by curiosity and a deep interest in technology.

My Projects




Patient Churn Prediction and Recommendations

Patient Churn Prediction and Recommendations is a system that uses machine learning to identify patients likely to stop visiting a hospital or clinic. It analyzes patient data and provides actionable recommendations to improve retention through targeted follow-ups and personalized care strategies.

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JS, Node.js

Government Scheme Finder

The Government Scheme Finder (myScheme) helps citizens discover schemes they’re eligible for. It covers central and state schemes in areas like agriculture, health, education, and welfare. Distribution of schemes and funds varies by state, with platforms like e-Utthaan showing financial allocations.

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JS, Node.js
Live Demo

AgriConnect Platform

Agri Connect is a digital platform that bridges the gap between farmers and agricultural service providers. It enables farmers to rent equipment like tractors, water pumps, and sprayers, and hire skilled labor with ease. The platform aims to boost agricultural productivity and reduce operational challenges in rural areas.

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JS, Node.js
